Art Pages 2.0: (coming soon!)

When complete artpages 2.0 will act as a free fully-functional website builder designed specifically to allow visual artists to easily build and maintain professional websites without monthly hosting fees. The platform is currently being coded and is planned for a limited beta launch during 2019. After it's launch artpages 2.0 will allow users to:

  1. login and edit their website independently
  2. seamlessly use their personal domain names / website url's
  3. change formatting, fonts etc. on their page with instant results
  4. add, remove and edit the order of their images live in browser
  5. add multiple fully customizable links and / or seperate pages
  6. add content to their page directly from their smart phone
  7. maintain their website for free indefinately
