Registering Your Website:

Securing the name or URL your website will use means registering a domain name. To get one of these you have to pay a small annual fee to a registration site. You shouldn't end up paying more than about $12 per year, if they try to charge you more than this try another registration site (linked below).

The most important thing is to make sure that you're only paying a yearly fee for registration not a monthly fee for hosting which I'll set up for you on the server space I'm managing.

In any case here are some good options:


dream-host

name-cheap (often discounted)

google-domains

go-daddy
